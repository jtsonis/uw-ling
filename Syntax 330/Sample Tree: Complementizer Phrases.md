- `[CP[C'[C][S[NP[NP[DP[D'[D The]]][N'[N dean's]]][N'[AP[A'[A mindless]]][N'[N secretary]]]][VP[V'[V asked][DP[D'[D me]]][CP[NP[DP[D'[D which]]][N'[N'[N professor][PP[P'[P of][NP[N'[N chemistry]]]]]][PP[P'[P from][NP[N'[N MIT]]]]]]][C'[C ø][S[DP[D'[D we]]][VP[V'[V'[V blamed]][PP[P'[P for][NP[DP[D'[D the]]][N'[N accident]]]]]]]]]]]]]]]` on www.mshang.ca/syntree

- As will be customary throughout the rest of the course, the top of the tree begins with what is now `[CP[C'][C][S]]` but which will soon be `[CP[C'[C][TP]]]`

- Observe that the V' to "asked" connects down to 3 other nodes. This is because "asked" is a **ditransitive** verb, meaning it has 3 total θ-roles.
